From the owners

We loved this house. We hope you will too.

When we bought this place, it was the third floor that did it — a wide, light-filled bonus floor with its own bedroom, bathroom, and flex room. It became our favorite part of the house.

What we didn't expect was how much we'd come to love the rest of it. The walk out our back gate onto Shelby Bottoms Greenway. Summer evenings at Cornelia Fort listening to bluegrass. Coffee runs to Ugly Mugs. The way our neighbors actually use the firepit on Friday nights.

This community was our haven through COVID and through my journey with secondary infertility. The people here have some of the biggest hearts and are some of the sweetest neighbors we've had the privilege to call friends. If that's the kind of community you want to come home to, this is where you should settle.

We're moving full-time to our small farm an hour north — a life change we've been working toward for years. The house is move-in ready and waiting for whoever's next. If that's you, we're glad it found you.

— Amy & Isaac

Out the Back Gate

Nearly 1,300 acres of parkland — right at your door.

Shelby Park & Bottoms is one of Nashville's largest urban parks, with direct access from East Greenway Park. Trails, river overlooks, a nature center, and a connection to Nashville's larger greenway system.

  • Community garden
  • Picnic areas with grills
  • Firepits to host social nights
  • Direct trail access to Shelby Bottoms
  • Cornelia Fort Airpark — concerts & events
  • Shelby Park Nature Center
  • 1,300 acres connecting to Nashville's larger greenway (great for bikers!)
